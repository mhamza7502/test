Islamic Classes

📖 Qur’an Studies

Embark on a sacred journey with the Qur’an — mastering the art of Tajweed, experiencing deep Tadabbur (reflection) on its verses, and understanding its timeless themes of Tawheed, Akhlaq, and Akhirah. Guided by the classical Hanafi tafsir tradition, students will not only recite beautifully but live the Qur’an with heart, humility, and devotion.

🔍 Hadith Studies

Immerse yourself in the blessed words of Rasulullah ﷺ, exploring the science of Hadith with sincerity and love. This course unveils authentic Hadiths, their spiritual meanings, and their relevance in our modern lives, nurturing within students the beauty of Sunnah, compassion, patience, and moral excellence.

Download Syllabus

⚖️ Islamic Fiqh

Strengthen your Islamic foundation by learning the rulings of worship, transactions, and everyday life, rooted in the balanced Hanafi Madhhab. Through a deep spiritual lens, students will discover the mercy behind Islamic law, correct common mistakes, and gain tools to live Islam with adab (etiquette), balance, and wisdom.

Download Syllabus

🕌 Islamic History

Journey through the golden ages of Islam — from the Prophetic mission to Andalusia to the Ottoman legacy — discovering leadership lessons, scientific advancements, cultural achievements, and revival efforts. This course builds identity, optimism, and pride in belonging to the Ummah of Rasulullah ﷺ.

Download Syllabus

Health Ethics in Islam

Study the Islamic principles guiding health, medical ethics, patient care, and professional conduct, following Islamic ethics and values.

⚕️ Medical Ethics

Understand Islamic rulings on patient rights, end-of-life care, confidentiality, and honesty in medical practice, inspired by traditional Fiqh and contemporary Islamic scholarship.

🤝 Professional Conduct

Learn the ethics of trustworthiness, compassion, and sincerity (ikhlas) as expected from Muslim healthcare professionals.

📚 Decision-Making

Explore Islamic approaches to bioethical dilemmas, informed consent, and balancing benefit vs harm (Maslahah vs Mafsadah).

Ashrafiyya Scholarship Program

Ashrafiyya supports students committed to the pursuit of Islamic knowledge. Scholarships are awarded based on sincerity, need, and dedication to lifelong learning, Insha'Allah.

🎓 Who Can Apply?

Students pursuing Qur'an, Hadith, Fiqh, and Islamic Ethics studies who demonstrate commitment and sincerity.

📝 Application Process

Fill out the scholarship application form. Provide motivation letter, background information, and recommendation.

📖 What is Covered?

Scholarships may cover full or partial tuition and provide access to mentorship and Islamic learning resources.
